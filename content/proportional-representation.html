What is Proportional Representation?

Proportional Representation is the idea that parties should hold seats in parliament in proportion to their popular vote.

40% of the vote? 40% of the seats. 15% of the vote? 15% of the seats.

We can measure voting system's proportionality with the Gallagher Index.
