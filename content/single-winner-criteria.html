Single Winner Voting Systems

There is no perfect single-winner voting system, and the hunt for one is like quicksand for the mind. Instead, take a step back and reconsider why we even use single-winner system especially in the context of legislative assemblies where proportional representation would better address the core concern of unfair representation. Investigating systems that result in a low Gallagher Index are likely a better use of your time.
